About us

We were born in 2020, a time when the world went inward. Established during the lockdown, Yoga.co.in sprouted from a desire to bring community and well-being virtually. We started with online yoga classes, fostering connection and movement in a way that fit the unprecedented circumstances.Since then, we've grown from a seed of possibility into a flourishing resource for yoga enthusiasts of all levels. With over 3 years of experience under our belt, we've cultivated a welcoming and knowledgeable online space. In fact, our dedication to innovation and inclusivity was recently recognized with a Guinness World Record certificate! We're proud to share this achievement as a testament to our commitment to making yoga accessible and transformative for everyone.

Our greatest testament lies in the experiences of our active members. We've cultivated a space where individuals of all backgrounds and abilities feel welcome. Sarah, a busy working mom, found yoga to be her escape, crediting our classes for helping her manage stress and improve her flexibility. John, a retiree, discovered a newfound sense of community and purpose through our interactive sessions. These are just a few of the countless stories that motivate us to keep growing and evolving. We believe that yoga is a practice for everyone, and we're here to empower you on your path to well-being. Join us, and let's find your center together.
